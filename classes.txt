Class: Plant

Attributes:
color (string)
flowerType (string)
leafCount (integer)
height (float)
alive (boolean)
thirsty (boolean)

Methods:
grow (adds 1 to height)
leafLoss (subtract 1 from leafCount)
leafGain (add 1 to leafCount)
absorbWater (thirsty value changes to no)
prune (subtract x from height)
